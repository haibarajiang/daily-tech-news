- [恶意 VSCode 扩展致 GitHub 3800 个仓库被入侵](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/github-confirms-breach-of-3-800-repos-via-malicious-vscode-extension/) (Hacker News)

- [Intuit 裁员超 3000 人以聚焦 AI](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/20/intuit-to-lay-off-over-3000-employees-to-refocus-on-ai/) (Hacker News)
